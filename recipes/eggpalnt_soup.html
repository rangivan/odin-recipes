Eggplant Soup

Description

Hearty Eggplant Soup made with ground meat and vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 lbs ground meat
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 lbs eggplant, diced
  • 3/4 cup sliced carrots
  • 3/4 cup sliced celery
  • 2 (14.5) cans Italian diced tomatoes, drained
  • 2 (14 ounce) cans broth
  • 1 tsp sugar (brown sugar)
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup dry macaroni
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 cup grated jParmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat, and cook the onion, ground meat, and garlic until beef is evenly brown.
  2. Drain grease, and mix in eggplant, carrots, celery, and tomatoes. Pour in beef broth. Mix in sugar, and season with nutmeg, salt and pepper.
  3. Cook and stir until heated through.
  4. Mix macaroni into the soup, and continue cooking 12 minutes, or until macaroni is al dente. Mix in parsley and top with Parmesan cheese to serve
